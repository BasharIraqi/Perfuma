Shipping and delivery policy

This policy will not apply to products from an external seller, as defined in section 4 of the site's regulations and terms of use

delivery options

Collection for the next business day

A. During the order, you can choose to pick up the products purchased on the Perfuma website on the next business day .

B. The cost of preparing the shipment for collection on the next business day will be NIS 10. For every order over NIS 99, shipping will be free of charge.

C. An order placed on Sunday-Wednesday starting at 20:00 will be considered as having been placed the next day and will therefore be delivered accordingly.

An order placed on Thursday - from 20:00 until Friday at 12:00 will be delivered on Sunday.

An order placed on Friday from 12:00 until Saturday at 20:00 will be delivered on Monday.

d. The products will arrive at the branch chosen by the customer, starting at 17:00 on the business day following the day on which the order was received, checked and confirmed (according to the business days of the relevant branch). The customer will receive a message (SMS message) to the phone number he entered when placing the order, when the order arrives at the aforementioned branch.

G. The customer must collect the products from the selected branch within 2 business days from the date the order is ready for collection, since if the customer does not arrive within the said time, Perfuma will be entitled to cancel the order (without charging the customer for the cost of the products) and charge the customer a handling fee of a total 14.90 NIS in addition to the cost of preparing the shipment (as applicable). In addition, in the aforementioned case, Perfuma will be entitled to prevent the customer from making future purchases through the website.

Next business day shipping

A. During the order, you can choose delivery via next business day delivery to the customer's home to an address in Israel that is included in the list of Perfumes's delivery areas only. If the customer wants the shipment to reach a locality where daily deliveries are not made, the delivery date will be determined according to the distribution dates of the delivery company, and in any case up to 7 business days from the day the order was placed. The list of localities to which delivery cannot be made from the Perfuma website is detailed in the above link.

B. Despite the above, it may not be possible to deliver deliveries to certain settlements in Israel (including according to the guidelines and policies of the external delivery company) even though they are included in the list of Perfuma's delivery areas.

C. Shipping cost -

C.1 For every order up to (including) 349 NIS, the shipping cost is 19.90 NIS.

C.2 For every order in the amount of over 349 NIS (prescription drugs will not be taken into account when calculating the amount of the order) shipping will be free of charge.
